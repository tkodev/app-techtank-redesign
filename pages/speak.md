# Speak — `/speak`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/speak
**Page title:** Our speakers are from the Toronto Tech Community — TechTank
**Role:** Recruit speakers / community educators for TechTank events.

---

## 1. Purpose

Convince members of the Toronto tech community to pitch a talk and give them
an easy way to submit.

## 2. Primary audience

- Practitioners who want public-speaking experience (devs, designers, PMs,
  researchers, founders)
- Community educators who want to teach something they're passionate about
- Speakers provided by sponsor companies

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank's speakers are drawn from the **Toronto tech community**.
- Monthly in-person meetups are oriented around a technical talk plus
  networking.
- The **Community Educators program** is a platform that invites members to
  teach a topic they love.
- TechTank can source speakers via its community reach, but also welcomes
  speakers provided by sponsor companies.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Speak at TechTank."
   - Sub-headline: "Share what you know with Toronto's tech community."
   - Primary CTA: "Submit a talk".

2. **Why speak**
   - Build your speaking portfolio.
   - Reach a friendly, inclusive, junior-friendly audience.
   - Get recorded (if applicable) and shared on YouTube / Guppy Talks.
   - Contribute to a diverse community.

3. **What we're looking for**
   - Technical topics (web, data, AI/ML, devtools, systems, security, etc.)
   - Career / industry insights (e.g., junior → mid-level strategies).
   - Accessible / intermediate-level framing preferred, but not required.
   - Workshops, panels, lightning talks, and full talks all welcome.

4. **Formats**
   - In-person monthly meetup (30–45 min talk + Q&A).
   - Guppy Talks (virtual panel series).
   - Study Tank (virtual technical deep-dive).
   - Community Educators (structured teaching session).

5. **What TechTank provides**
   - Venue (via sponsor host) and AV setup for in-person.
   - Event promotion across Meetup, LinkedIn, Luma, Slack, Instagram.
   - Event coordination and MCing.

6. **Submission form (or clear CTA)**
   - Fields: name, email, role/company, proposed title, abstract, target
     audience, preferred format, bio, LinkedIn, availability.
   - Alternative: mailto `techtankto@gmail.com` with a template.

7. **Past speakers / highlights (optional)**
   - Embed YouTube recordings or quotes.

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Submit a talk proposal.
2. Contact `techtankto@gmail.com`.
3. Attend an event first (`/events`).

## 6. Functional requirements

- Submission form with spam protection.
- Confirmation page / email on submit.
- Open Graph metadata optimized for recruiting speakers (shareable card).

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A would-be speaker can understand the formats and submit a pitch in under
  two minutes.
- Page makes clear that speakers come from the community — not just paid
  professionals — lowering the barrier for first-timers.
