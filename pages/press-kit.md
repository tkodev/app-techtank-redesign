# Press Kit — `/press-kit`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/press-kit
**Page title:** Press Kit — TechTank TO
**Role:** Standalone resource page for media, partners, and sponsors.
Houses brand guidelines, logos, fast-facts, and boilerplate.

---

## 1. Purpose

Give journalists, sponsors, hosts, and partners a one-stop place to
grab correctly-licensed TechTank assets and a reliable paragraph of
copy they can quote. Also a trust-builder on its own — having a real
press kit signals that the community is serious.

## 2. Primary audience

- Journalists and bloggers covering Toronto tech
- Prospective sponsors researching before first contact
- Hosts putting together internal decks
- Event co-marketing partners

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank is Toronto's inclusive, volunteer-run tech community,
  founded in 2022.
- All assets on this page are free to use with attribution.
- Please don't modify the logo or use brand colors outside the
  guidelines.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "TechTank Press Kit".
   - Sub-headline: "Logos, guidelines, and fast facts for press,
     sponsors, and partners."
   - Primary CTA: "Download all assets (ZIP)".

2. **Fast facts**
   - Founded: 2022
   - Location: Toronto, Canada
   - Cadence: monthly in-person events + ongoing Slack community
   - Typical attendance: 100–120 / event
   - Volunteer-run, non-commercial
   - Contact: `techtankto@gmail.com`
   - Finalize all numbers with organizers before publishing.

3. **Boilerplate**
   - A 1-paragraph description partners can paste into their press
     releases or decks, verbatim.
   - A 1-sentence short version for captions / bios.

4. **Logos**
   - Primary logo (full color, on light, on dark).
   - Monogram / fish mark.
   - Wordmark.
   - SVG + PNG (1x, 2x, 3x) downloads.
   - Usage guidance: minimum clear space, minimum size, don'ts.

5. **Brand guidelines**
   - Linked PDF with colors (hex / RGB), type pairing, photography
     style, voice.
   - Inline preview of the core palette.

6. **Event photography**
   - Curated, rights-cleared photos ready for press use.
   - Credit line included per image.

7. **Event support templates**
   - Branded Google Slides / PPTX speaker template.
   - Branded social post templates.
   - Host checklist and run-of-show PDFs.

8. **Contact**
   - Press contact email.
   - Note that interviews / quotes should be arranged in advance.

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Download all assets (ZIP)
2. Email `techtankto@gmail.com` for press / partnership inquiries
3. Visit `/how-it-works/sponsor` for corporate support

## 6. Functional requirements

- All downloads served from reliable static storage with long cache
  headers.
- ZIP bundle contains logos, guidelines PDF, boilerplate, and a
  README.
- Logo images include alt text describing the variant.
- Usage terms visible before download.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A partner can grab a usable logo and a one-paragraph description in
  under a minute, without emailing anyone.
- No asset on the page is a broken link.
- The page clearly states what is and isn't allowed regarding
  modification and commercial use.
