# Privacy Policy — `/legal/privacy-policy`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/legal/privacy-policy
**Page title:** Privacy Policy — TechTank TO
**Role:** Explains what personal information TechTank collects, how
it's used, and who it's shared with.

---

## 1. Purpose

Be transparent about TechTank's minimal-PII stance. The community
collects almost nothing directly; most data lives with external
platforms (Luma, Meetup, Google Forms, Slack).

## 2. Primary audience

- Website visitors
- Event attendees
- Intake-form submitters (speaker, host, sponsor, volunteer)

## 3. Structure

Standard privacy document, rendered in the `/legal` shared layout with
a narrow column, table of contents sidebar, and a last-updated stamp.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Preamble**
   - Effective date, last-updated date.
   - Scope: `techtankto.com` and TechTank-operated community channels.

2. **Data we collect directly**
   - Intake-form submissions (via Google Forms): name, email, optional
     profile links, role-specific info (talk abstract, company, etc.).
   - Contact emails you send to `techtankto@gmail.com`.
   - Basic analytics (see below).

3. **Data we don't collect**
   - No accounts, no passwords, no payment data on-site.
   - No tracking cookies beyond what analytics requires.

4. **Third-party platforms**
   - Explicit list of platforms and what they process:
     - **Meetup / Luma** — RSVPs, attendee lists.
     - **Google Forms** — intake submissions.
     - **Slack** — community messages.
     - **YouTube, Instagram, LinkedIn, GitHub** — embedded content.
   - Each platform has its own privacy policy; links included.

5. **Analytics**
   - Tool used (e.g. Plausible) and that it is privacy-respecting.
   - What is measured (aggregate page views, referrers) and what is
     not (individuals, cross-site tracking).

6. **How we use the data**
   - Respond to intake submissions.
   - Send event-related follow-ups.
   - Improve the website and community programs.

7. **Sharing**
   - We do not sell data.
   - Limited sharing with host companies (e.g. attendee lists on their
     request, per event).

8. **Retention**
   - Intake submissions kept while the conversation is active + a
     reasonable archival window.
   - Analytics aggregated and retained per the analytics tool's policy.

9. **Your rights**
   - Request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing
     `techtankto@gmail.com`.
   - Rights available under PIPEDA (Canada) and, where applicable,
     GDPR.

10. **Cookies**
    - Whether the site sets cookies and what for.

11. **Changes to this policy**
    - Updates announced via the site; material changes highlighted.

12. **Contact**
    - `techtankto@gmail.com`.

## 5. Functional requirements

- Shared `/legal` layout with table of contents.
- Last-updated timestamp rendered from source metadata.
- Cookie banner implemented only if analytics/embeds require it.

## 6. Acceptance criteria

- The document honestly reflects what the site and community actually
  collect — no boilerplate that implies features we don't have.
- Reviewed by a volunteer organizer with legal familiarity before
  publication.
