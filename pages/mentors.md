# Mentors — `/mentors`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/mentors
**Page title:** Mentors — TechTank
**Role:** Recruit mentors for TechTank's mentorship program.

---

## 1. Purpose

Convince experienced tech professionals to sign up as mentors and give them a
frictionless way to apply.

## 2. Primary audience

- Mid-to-senior tech professionals (devs, designers, PMs, founders) in or
  near Toronto
- People who want to give back, recruit, or grow their network
- Entrepreneurs and domain experts interested in sharing ideas

## 3. Key messages

- "Unlock the joy of mentoring in tech" — inspire and guide aspiring minds,
  share your expertise, shape the future of tech.
- TechTank provides:
  - Hands-on learning via workshops, discussions, and social events
  - A vibrant network of learners and innovators
  - A community grounded in diversity and inclusion
  - Opportunities for growth through mentorship and networking
- Welcomes: mentors, recruiters, collaborators, and entrepreneurs.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Become a mentor with TechTank."
   - Sub-headline: mission-driven pitch.
   - Primary CTA: "Apply to mentor".

2. **Why mentor with us**
   - Bullet list of benefits:
     - Inspire / guide junior talent.
     - Expand your network in the Toronto tech community.
     - Contribute to a diverse, inclusive tech ecosystem.
     - Recruit or collaborate with passionate learners.

3. **What mentorship looks like**
   - Format(s): 1:1, small group, panels, event-based.
   - Time commitment: flexible (describe typical cadence).
   - Topics: technical, career, networking, interviews.

4. **Who we're looking for**
   - Industry professionals at any stage past junior.
   - Entrepreneurs, specialists, and hiring managers welcome.
   - No teaching experience required.

5. **How it works**
   - Apply → intro call / onboarding → matched / invited to events.

6. **Application form (or clear CTA)**
   - Fields: name, email, role, company (optional), areas of expertise,
     availability, LinkedIn / portfolio, why you want to mentor.
   - Alternative: mailto `techtankto@gmail.com` with a subject template.

7. **Related programs**
   - Community Educators (`/activities` / `/speak`)
   - Guppy Talks panels
   - Host Us (`/host`) for companies

8. **FAQ (optional)**
   - Is there a fee? (No.)
   - Do I need to be in Toronto? (Hybrid / virtual possible.)
   - How are mentees matched?

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Apply to mentor.
2. Contact `techtankto@gmail.com` for questions.
3. Attend an event (`/events`) to meet the community first.

## 6. Functional requirements

- Application form with spam protection (honeypot / CAPTCHA).
- Form submissions routed to `techtankto@gmail.com` or a CRM.
- Confirmation page / email on submit.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A qualified professional can go from landing on the page to submitting an
  application in under two minutes.
- The page clearly distinguishes mentor intake from speaker intake
  (`/speak`) and host/sponsor intake (`/host`).
