# Speaker — `/how-it-works/speaker`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/how-it-works/speaker
**Page title:** Speak at TechTank — TechTank TO
**Role:** Convince qualified technologists to speak at a TechTank event
and capture their intake.

---

## 1. Purpose

Give a potential speaker everything they need to say yes in one scroll:
audience, format, logistics, what they get, and how to sign up.

## 2. Primary audience

- Engineers, designers, PMs, founders in Toronto tech
- First-time and returning speakers

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank speakers come from the Toronto tech community.
- We help first-time speakers succeed.
- 30–45 minute talk + Q&A; any tech topic.
- The talk is recorded and published to YouTube — you keep the recording.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Speak at a TechTank event."
   - Sub-headline: "Share what you know with the Toronto tech community."
   - Primary CTA: "Sign up to speak" → intake form.
   - Supporting image: real event photo of a speaker.

2. **Why speak**
   - **Improve your speaking skills** — we help first-time speakers.
   - **Market your skills** — talks are recorded and distributed.
   - **Karma** — the community runs on people sharing what they know.

3. **Logistics**
   - **Talk duration:** 30–45 minutes plus Q&A.
   - **Topics:** anything related to tech.
   - **Format:** in-person at a host venue in Toronto.
   - **Audience:** typically 100–120 attendees per event.
   - **Recording:** published to TechTank's YouTube channel.

4. **What TechTank handles**
   - Venue and catering (via a host company).
   - Marketing (Meetup, Slack, LinkedIn, Instagram).
   - Run-of-show coordination and MCing.
   - Recording and post-production.
   - Optional: feedback / rehearsal sessions for first-time speakers.

5. **What you provide**
   - A talk proposal (title + abstract + bio).
   - Slides using our template (optional but recommended).
   - Yourself, on event night.

6. **Speaker resources**
   - Branded slide deck template (Google Slides / PPTX) — link.
   - Speaker run-of-show PDF — link.
   - Tips for first-time speakers — link or inline.

7. **Testimonials**
   - 2–3 quotes from past speakers with photo, role, and talk title.

8. **Intake form**
   - Embedded Google Form (or link) with fields:
     - Name, email, LinkedIn
     - Proposed talk title, abstract (300 chars), length
     - Short bio
     - Speaker experience (first time / some / experienced)
     - Preferred month(s) / willing to wait
     - Anything else

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Submit speaker intake form
2. Download the slide template
3. Join our Slack (for questions)

## 6. Functional requirements

- Intake form embedded if technically feasible, otherwise linked
  with a clear button.
- Slide template link opens in a new tab.
- Social proof (past speakers + YouTube recordings) must appear above
  the intake form.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A potential speaker can understand the commitment and sign up without
  leaving the page.
- First-time speakers see explicit reassurance that we help them
  prepare.
