# Donation Terms & Conditions — `/terms-conditions`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/terms-conditions
**Page title:** Donation Terms & Conditions — TechTank
**Role:** Legal page outlining how donations to TechTank are handled.

---

## 1. Purpose

Provide a clear, legally-sound description of how donations are used,
collected, and stored so donors can make informed decisions.

## 2. Primary audience

- Prospective donors doing due diligence
- Legal / finance reviewers at donor companies
- TechTank administrators (single source of truth)

## 3. Key terms to include (from current site content)

1. **Use of Donations**
   All donations made towards TechTank will be used at the discretion of the
   administrators of TechTank within stated objectives.

2. **Nature of the Donation**
   All donors understand and agree that donations do **not entail a
   transactional exchange** with TechTank. Donations are **non-refundable**.

3. **Payment Methods**
   - **Interac E-Transfer** to `techtankto@gmail.com`.
   - **Stripe** for credit / debit card payments.

4. **Data Privacy**
   - Personally identifiable information may be collected in order to
     account for and identify donation transactions.
   - Interac data is securely stored within the financial institutions
     sending and receiving the donation — **no information stored externally
     by TechTank**.
   - Stripe data is securely stored within Stripe — **no information stored
     externally by TechTank**.

5. **Amendments**
   TechTank reserves the right to amend the Donation Terms & Conditions at
   any time without prior notice.

6. **Governing Law & Jurisdiction**
   All terms and conditions are governed by the laws of **Ontario, Canada**
   and are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts within
   **Toronto, Ontario, Canada**.

7. **Contact**
   For questions or concerns, contact the administrators at
   `techtankto@gmail.com`.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Header**
   - Page title.
   - Last-updated date.

2. **Sections 1–7** as above, each with a clear heading.

3. **Footer**
   - Link back to `/donate`.
   - Contact email.

## 5. Functional requirements

- Plain HTML / markdown rendering; no forms, no interactive elements.
- Anchor links on each section heading for deep-linking.
- Printable (avoid background images that don't print well).
- Include a visible **"Last updated: YYYY-MM-DD"** stamp.

## 6. Acceptance criteria

- The wording matches the canonical terms on file with TechTank
  administrators.
- A link to this page is present on `/donate` and in the site footer.
- No PII is collected on this page itself.
