# Host Us — `/host`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/host
**Page title:** Host Us — TechTank
**Role:** Recruit companies to sponsor / host TechTank's monthly in-person
events by providing venue and food.

---

## 1. Purpose

Make it easy for Toronto-area companies to understand why and how to host a
TechTank event, and to get in touch.

## 2. Primary audience

- Toronto-based tech companies / startups
- People-ops, talent/recruiting, DevRel, marketing leads
- Community managers at co-working spaces

## 3. Key messages

- Every month, TechTank hosts an in-person meetup or community event
  **oriented around a technical talk** plus networking/mingling before and
  after.
- TechTank **relies on sponsorship from Toronto companies** to provide
  **venue and food**.
- The host company gets **marketing across Meetup, Slack, and LinkedIn**.
- TechTank handles speakers, marketing, coordination; the host provides
  space and catering.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Host a TechTank event."
   - Sub-headline: "Bring the Toronto tech community to your space."
   - Primary CTA: "Contact us to host".

2. **What hosting looks like**
   - Monthly in-person meetup: 2–3 hours, evenings.
   - Typical flow: arrival + mingling → technical talk → Q&A → networking.
   - Expected audience size (range, to be filled in).

3. **What TechTank provides**
   - Speakers (sourced from the community or provided by the host).
   - Marketing across Meetup, Slack, LinkedIn, Instagram.
   - Event coordination and MCing.
   - Promotion of the host company's brand.

4. **What the host provides**
   - Venue with seating + AV (projector / screen / mic).
   - Food and drinks.
   - Optional: a speaker from the host company.
   - Optional: any marketing materials to share.

5. **What you get as a host**
   - Brand exposure to Toronto's junior and mid-level tech talent.
   - Pipeline for recruiting (tasteful, not pushy).
   - Positive association with an inclusive community.

6. **Process**
   - Initial contact → scoping call → date, speaker and logistics →
     marketing kickoff → event day.

7. **Contact / intake**
   - Form or mailto `techtankto@gmail.com`.
   - Fields: company, contact, role, proposed venue, capacity, preferred
     month(s), any speaker they want to feature.

8. **FAQ (optional)**
   - Is there a sponsorship fee? (No — you provide venue + food.)
   - Can we record the talk? (Typically yes, with speaker consent.)
   - Can we bring our own speaker? (Yes — we can collaborate.)

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Contact to host.
2. Email `techtankto@gmail.com`.

## 6. Functional requirements

- Form or mailto with a clear template.
- Social proof: logos of past hosting companies (if available).
- Photos / short video from past events.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A prospective host understands, in under a minute, what they provide and
  what TechTank provides.
- Contact flow is obvious and one-click from the hero.
