# Activities — `/activities`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/activities
**Page title:** Activities — TechTank
**Role:** Catalog of the ongoing programs TechTank runs beyond one-off events.

---

## 1. Purpose

Explain the recurring programs (the "what we actually do") so visitors can
pick the one that fits them and follow the link to participate.

## 2. Primary audience

- Members deciding where to plug in
- First-time visitors curious about what regular programming looks like
- Potential collaborators looking to partner on a specific program

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank offers a variety of activities designed to enhance networking and
  learning in the Toronto tech community.
- Programs serve different vibes: deep technical talks, casual socials,
  structured learning, and community teaching.

## 4. Content sections

Each program gets its own block with: name, short description, audience,
cadence, and where to join.

1. **Tech Tank Socials**
   - Non-coding community meetups: networking, team-building, social hangouts.
   - Cadence: monthly / as scheduled.
   - Join via: Meetup / Luma.

2. **Guppy Talks (Virtual Panel Discussion Series)**
   - Panel discussions aimed at supporting early-career devs and
     tech-enthusiasts — confidence-building, job search, networking tactics.
   - Also exists as a **podcast** (bite-sized tech knowledge).
   - Join via: Luma RSVP, YouTube, podcast platforms.

3. **Mentorship Program**
   - Connects aspiring tech enthusiasts with industry experts for insights,
     skill development, and networking.
   - CTA → `/mentors`.

4. **Study Tank**
   - Virtual initiative to discuss software development topics: data
     structures, algorithms, web fundamentals, code reviews, projects.
   - Audience: self-learners and early-career devs.

5. **Code Diversity Coffee Chat**
   - Monthly coffee chat / social for Women & Non-Binary folks in tech.
   - Partnered events (e.g., with 7shifts) hosted on Luma.

6. **Community Educators Program**
   - Platform inviting community members to teach a topic they're
     passionate about.
   - CTA → `/speak` for submitting a topic.

7. **Monthly in-person meetups**
   - Technical talks + networking, hosted at sponsor venues.
   - Link to `/events` and `/host`.

## 5. Calls to action

- RSVP to relevant events (`/events`).
- Apply to mentor (`/mentors`).
- Propose a talk (`/speak`).
- Host a program (`/host`).

## 6. Functional requirements

- Each program block should link to: an RSVP destination, a contact method,
  and (if applicable) a recurring landing page on Meetup / Luma.
- Support adding new programs over time with minimal design effort.
- Provide icons / visuals consistent with the aquatic brand motif.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A visitor can identify, in under a minute, which program matches their
  interest (learning, networking, teaching, mentorship, diversity).
- Each program has at least one clear next-step link.
