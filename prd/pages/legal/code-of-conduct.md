# Code of Conduct — `/legal/code-of-conduct`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/legal/code-of-conduct
**Page title:** Code of Conduct — TechTank TO
**Role:** The community's expectations for behavior at events and in
community channels, and how to report violations.

---

## 1. Purpose

Make explicit that TechTank is an inclusive, safe community; set
concrete expectations; and provide a clear, low-friction path to report
issues. This document operationalizes the **Respect** pillar from
`/about`.

## 2. Primary audience

- All attendees at TechTank events
- All members of TechTank-operated channels (Slack, etc.)
- Speakers, hosts, sponsors, and volunteers

## 3. Structure

Rendered in the `/legal` shared layout with a narrow column, table of
contents sidebar, and a last-updated stamp. Linked prominently from
`/about`, `/events`, and every `/get-involved/*` page.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Preamble**
   - TechTank is a diverse, inclusive community. This Code applies to
     all TechTank spaces (online and in-person) and to anyone
     representing TechTank.

2. **Expected behavior**
   - Be respectful and considerate.
   - Assume good intent; de-escalate when possible.
   - Use inclusive language.
   - Respect privacy — do not share photos or quotes of others
     without consent.
   - Give credit and attribution.

3. **Unacceptable behavior**
   - Harassment in any form (verbal, physical, visual, digital).
   - Discrimination based on race, gender identity, sexual
     orientation, disability, age, religion, nationality, or any
     other protected attribute.
   - Intimidation, stalking, or following.
   - Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
   - Disruption of talks, events, or online discussions.
   - Retaliation against someone who reports a violation.

4. **Scope**
   - Applies at events, in Slack, on associated social channels, and
     anywhere else TechTank operates.
   - Applies equally to attendees, speakers, hosts, sponsors,
     volunteers, and organizers.

5. **Reporting**
   - At events: speak to any TechTank organizer on site.
   - Any time: email `techtankto@gmail.com`.
   - Reports are handled confidentially and as fast as reasonably
     possible.

6. **Response and consequences**
   - Possible responses: private conversation, formal warning,
     removal from an event, removal from community channels, ban.
   - Consequences are proportional to the behavior and its impact.

7. **Acknowledgements**
   - Draws on widely-used community codes (e.g. Contributor Covenant,
     Berlin Code of Conduct); license them appropriately.

8. **Contact**
   - `techtankto@gmail.com`.

## 5. Functional requirements

- Shared `/legal` layout with table of contents.
- Linked from primary nav / footer and from `/about`, `/events`,
  `/get-involved/*`.
- Last-updated timestamp rendered from source metadata.
- License notice rendered at the bottom if the document derives from a
  licensed template.

## 6. Acceptance criteria

- A reader can find the reporting email in under 5 seconds from the
  top of the page.
- The document is the single canonical source — no conflicting
  versions elsewhere on the site.
- Reviewed by organizers before publication.
