# Host — `/get-involved/host`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/get-involved/host
**Page title:** Host a TechTank Event — TechTank TO
**Role:** Recruit Toronto companies to provide venue and food for a
TechTank monthly meetup.

---

## 1. Purpose

Make it obvious what's required to host a TechTank event and how to
start the conversation. This is a conversion page: the "Contact us to
host" action is the single most important CTA.

## 2. Primary audience

- People-ops, DevRel, marketing, and talent leads at Toronto tech
  companies
- Community managers at co-working spaces

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank is volunteer-run; hosts provide **venue and food**.
- Events run **6:00pm – 8:30pm**, typically **40–100 attendees**.
- The host company gets brand visibility and recruiting pipeline with
  zero salesy vibe.
- TechTank handles speakers, marketing, and coordination end-to-end.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Host a TechTank event."
   - Sub-headline: "Bring the Toronto tech community to your space."
   - Primary CTA: "Contact us to host" → email us (`techtankto@gmail.com`).
   - Visual: real photo from a past hosted event.

2. **Why host**
   - **Recruiting** — reach Toronto tech talent in a non-pushy way.
   - **Back to the office** — a reason for your team to come in.
   - **Karma** — the tech community is interrelated; support it.

3. **Event logistics**
   - **Capacity:** 40–100 attendees.
   - **Timing:** Start at 6:00pm, wrap by 8:30pm.
   - **Location:** must be reasonably accessible by TTC subway or
     streetcar.
   - **AV:** projector + screen required; microphone nice-to-have.
   - **Food:** pizza + non-alcoholic drinks is the baseline.
   - **Recording:** TechTank records the talk and posts it to YouTube.

4. **What TechTank handles**
   - Speaker sourcing (unless you want to bring your own).
   - Marketing (Slack, LinkedIn, Instagram).
   - Registration and attendee tracking.
   - Event coordination and MCing.
   - Recording + publishing to YouTube.

5. **What you provide**
   - Venue with seating and AV.
   - Food and drinks.
   - Optional: your own speaker.
   - Optional: your marketing materials.

6. **What you get**
   - Logo on event marketing across channels.
   - Attendee list (post-event).
   - Brand visibility on YouTube and social.
   - Photos from the event (for your channels).

7. **Process**
   - Initial contact → scoping call → date + speaker + logistics →
     marketing kickoff → event day.

8. **Past hosts (social proof)**
   - Logo cloud of companies that have hosted.
   - 1–2 quotes from past host contacts.

9. **Host resources**
   - Host checklist PDF (AV, food, timing, setup).
   - Day-of run-of-show PDF.

10. **Intake action (email us)**
    - Prominent `mailto:techtankto@gmail.com` CTA with a pre-filled
      subject line ("Host inquiry — <company>") and a
      copy-to-clipboard scaffold suggesting:
      - Company, contact name, role, email
      - Proposed venue + capacity
      - TTC accessibility notes
      - Preferred month(s)
      - Whether you'd like to provide a speaker
      - Anything else

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Email `techtankto@gmail.com` to propose hosting
2. Read the host checklist
3. Visit `/press-kit` for brand context

## 6. Functional requirements

- Prominent intake CTA (`mailto:techtankto@gmail.com`) with a clear
  button and suggested-details scaffold.
- Host checklist and run-of-show are real downloadable PDFs, not
  placeholders.
- Page includes visible past-host logos and event photos.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A prospective host understands, in under one minute, what they
  provide vs. what TechTank provides.
- The capacity (40–100) and timing (6:00–8:30pm) are visible without
  hunting.
