# Volunteer — `/how-it-works/volunteer`

**URL:** https://www.techtankto.com/how-it-works/volunteer
**Page title:** Volunteer with TechTank — TechTank TO
**Role:** Onboard the crew that keeps TechTank running — organizers,
event-day volunteers, content volunteers, community moderators.

---

## 1. Purpose

Make it explicit that TechTank is built by volunteers, describe the
roles available, and make signing up low-friction.

## 2. Primary audience

- Community members who attend events and want to give back
- Early-career folks looking to build experience organizing
- People with specialized skills (photography, video, design, social)

## 3. Key messages

- TechTank is 100% volunteer-run.
- There are many ways to help — not all require public speaking or
  heavy time commitments.
- We welcome people at all skill levels and stages.

## 4. Content sections

1. **Hero**
   - Headline: "Help run TechTank."
   - Sub-headline: "Join the crew that makes Toronto's tech community
     happen every month."
   - Primary CTA: "Sign up to volunteer" → email us (`techtankto@gmail.com`).

2. **Why volunteer**
   - **Community** — meet people doing the same thing you are.
   - **Skills** — organize real events, build portfolio work.
   - **Karma** — community runs on showing up.

3. **Volunteer roles**
   - **Event-day crew** — check-in, setup, teardown, greeting attendees.
   - **Photography / video** — capture the events for socials.
   - **Social media** — draft posts, repost speaker slides, Instagram.
   - **Design** — event graphics, social assets, slide templates.
   - **Community moderation** — keep Slack welcoming and on-topic.
   - **Organizing** — help plan future events, outreach, speaker
     sourcing.

4. **Time commitment**
   - Event-day: ~3–4 hours on the night of an event, once a month or
     less.
   - Ongoing roles (social, design, moderation): 1–3 hours/week.
   - Organizer roles: variable; onboarding conversation first.

5. **What you get**
   - A front-row seat in Toronto's tech community.
   - A shout-out in credits where it makes sense.
   - Portfolio-quality work (for photographers/designers especially).
   - The gratitude of an entire tech community.

6. **Current crew (optional)**
   - Photos + first names of current volunteers as social proof.

7. **Intake action (email us)**
   - Prominent `mailto:techtankto@gmail.com` CTA with a pre-filled
     subject line ("Volunteer — <your name>") and a
     copy-to-clipboard scaffold suggesting:
     - Name, email, LinkedIn (optional)
     - Roles you're interested in
     - Availability (weeknight events OK? ongoing hours?)
     - Relevant skills / portfolio link
     - Anything else

## 5. Calls to action (priority order)

1. Email `techtankto@gmail.com` to volunteer
2. Join our Slack
3. Come to an event first (link to `/events`)

## 6. Functional requirements

- Prominent intake CTA (`mailto:techtankto@gmail.com`) with a clear
  button and suggested-details scaffold.
- Role list renders from structured content so organizers can edit it.
- "Come to an event first" path is always offered for people not ready
  to commit.

## 7. Acceptance criteria

- A visitor can find a volunteer role that matches a non-organizing
  skill (e.g. photography) without needing to ask.
- The time commitment per role is stated up-front.
